Churn Prediction

The main objective of this project is to develop a churn predicton model which assist telecom operators to predict customers who are most likely to churn.

About

LOGISTIC REGRESSION

By applying logistic regression algorithm, classification and estimation model were successfully built. With this model, the telelcom operators will be able to predict customers churn, which is one of the most important concerns for larger companies.
